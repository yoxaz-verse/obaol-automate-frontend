# Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy explains how **OBAOL Supreme** collects, uses, stores, and protects information when you access or engage with our platform, ecosystem, and services.

By using OBAOL, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

---

## 1. About OBAOL Supreme

OBAOL Supreme operates as a **verified agro trade ecosystem** supporting real-world agricultural and commodity trade.

We are not a marketplace, advertising network, or data brokerage platform.  
We collect only the information required to maintain trust, verification, and execution discipline within the ecosystem.

---

## 2. Information We Collect

We may collect the following categories of information:

### a. Information You Provide Directly
- Name, phone number, email address  
- Business name and role  
- Trade-related information shared voluntarily  
- Verification-related details (as applicable)

### b. Trade & Engagement Information
- Participation within the ecosystem  
- Communication and engagement records  
- Execution-related coordination data

### c. Technical & Usage Information
- IP address and device information  
- Browser type and usage patterns  
- Access logs for security and performance

---

## 3. Purpose of Data Collection

We collect information strictly for the following purposes:

- Participant verification and ecosystem integrity  
- Facilitating communication and engagement  
- Supporting trade execution and coordination  
- Improving platform performance and reliability  
- Security, fraud prevention, and compliance  

We do **not** collect personal data for advertising or resale.

---

## 4. Verification & Trust

Verification is a core part of the OBAOL ecosystem.

Information collected during verification:
- Is used only to assess legitimacy and readiness  
- Is not publicly disclosed without consent  
- Is not used for unrelated purposes  

Verification does not guarantee trade outcomes and does not replace independent due diligence.

---

## 5. Use of AI, Automation & Technology

OBAOL uses technology, automation, and AI-assisted tools to:

- Improve communication quality  
- Support engagement workflows  
- Enhance coordination efficiency  

AI systems:
- Do not make autonomous trade decisions  
- Do not determine pricing or commercial terms  
- Operate under human oversight  

Data used for automation remains within controlled and secure environments.

---

## 6. Sharing of Information

OBAOL does not sell personal data.

Information may be shared only:
- With verified participants involved in a specific trade context  
- With trusted service partners (logistics, packaging, technology) when required for execution  
- When legally required by applicable laws or authorities  

All sharing is limited to what is necessary for execution or compliance.

---

## 7. Data Security

We implement reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect information against:
- Unauthorized access  
- Misuse or alteration  
- Accidental loss  

However, no digital system can guarantee absolute security. Participants are encouraged to exercise caution when sharing sensitive information.

---

## 8. Data Retention

Information is retained only as long as necessary to:
- Maintain ecosystem integrity  
- Support trade execution and compliance  
- Meet legal or regulatory requirements  

We periodically review stored data and remove information that is no longer required.

---

## 9. Cookies & Tracking

OBAOL may use basic cookies or similar technologies to:
- Maintain session continuity  
- Improve usability  
- Monitor platform performance  

We do not use cookies for third-party advertising or behavioral tracking.
