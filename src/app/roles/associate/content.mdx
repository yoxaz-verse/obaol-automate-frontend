# Associate Role Directory — Who Can Be an Associate

Associates are **registered companies and business entities** that execute trade and trade-support operations on OBAOL Supreme.

## Full Associate Ecosystem
OBAOL supports dedicated role pages for:

- **Traders**
- **Importers**
- **Exporters**
- **Warehouse Owners**
- **Inland Transportation**
- **Freight Forwarders**
- **Logistics Providers**
- **Suppliers**
- **Packaging Companies**
- **Quality Testing Labs**
- **Agritech Companies**
- **Customs Clearance Agencies**
- **Finance & Insurance Partners**
- **Procurement Partners**

## What Associates Do on OBAOL
Associates use the platform to:
- Manage enquiries and execution milestones
- Coordinate logistics and movement across partners
- Align documentation and compliance workflows
- Participate in a single, integrated trade ecosystem

## Why This Directory Matters
Each associate type has its own dedicated subpage so users can quickly understand:
- Who can join
- What role they play in execution
- How OBAOL supports that role
