## What This Is

This is not a job opening.

OBAOL operates as a **trade execution system** in the agro market.

Inside this system, individuals operate as **Digital Agro Traders (Operators)**.

An Operator is someone who:

- Identifies real trade opportunities
- Builds supplier and buyer relationships
- Coordinates during execution
- Stays involved until completion

## What Makes This Different

Most people in trade:

- Share prices
- Forward contacts
- Drop off after initial discussion

Operators:

- Stay involved
- Handle coordination
- Focus on execution, not just discussion

This difference is where value is created.

## How OBAOL Fits In

OBAOL is not a marketplace.
We do not act as traders.
We do not provide leads.

We work as an **execution layer** alongside trade participants.

We support:

- Opportunity validation
- Counterparty verification
- Execution tracking

Operators use this system while working on real trades.

## How You Operate

As an Operator:

- You do not own inventory
- You do not take price positions
- You work between real demand and supply

Your role is to:

- Move deals forward
- Coordinate between parties
- Ensure completion

## Earning Structure (Controlled Clarity)

Earnings are linked to **completed trades only**.

When a trade is successfully executed:

- A commission pool is created
- It is distributed among contributors

This includes:

- Deal execution (closing the trade)
- Supplier ownership
- Supporting layers

No earnings are generated from:

- Inquiries
- Negotiations
- Partial progress

Only completed execution matters.

## Reality Check

This is simple in structure, but not easy in execution.

It requires:

- Consistent follow-up
- Coordination across multiple parties
- Staying involved until closure

This is not passive participation.

## Two Ways To Operate

You can participate in two ways:

### 1. Independent Operator

You operate on your own.

- Build your own relationships
- Handle coordination independently
- Use the system when required

Suitable if you already understand trade.

### 2. Team-Based Operator

You operate within a structured team.

- Work alongside others
- Get practical guidance
- Learn execution while operating

Suitable if you are building your capability.

There is no cost for this.
