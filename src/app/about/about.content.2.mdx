## The Reality of Commodity Trade

In Indian commodity markets, discovery is rarely the real problem.

Most established traders already have:

- Long-standing contacts  
- Active buyers and suppliers  
- Market knowledge  
- Negotiation capability  

Yet trades still collapse.

**Why?**

Because execution is fragile.

Common breakdown points include:

- Unverified or misrepresented counterparties  
- False availability or inflated demand  
- Price manipulation to extract information  
- Poor coordination after terms are agreed  

This is the gap OBAOL is built to address.

## How OBAOL Works

OBAOL functions as an **extension of your sales and execution team**.

Our involvement follows a disciplined flow:

### 1. Opportunity Identification  
Relevant trade opportunities are actively identified.  
Not through open listings, but through targeted, intent-driven sourcing.

### 2. Seriousness & Intent Filtering  
Both existing traders and new entrants are filtered for readiness, intent, and alignment.

### 3. Counterparty Verification  
Credibility and trade readiness are validated before execution begins.

### 4. Execution Coordination  
Support continues during negotiation, documentation, and on-ground coordination.

### 5. Trade Completion  
Our involvement remains until the trade successfully closes.
