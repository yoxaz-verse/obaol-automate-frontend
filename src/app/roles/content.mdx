# Operator vs Associate

## Associate (Company Role)
Associates represent **companies** using the OBAOL platform.  
If you run or represent a business in trade, logistics, warehousing, or related services, you are an **Associate**.

**Associates can be:**
- Traders (buyers/sellers)
- Logistics providers (inland, ocean, air, customs)
- Warehouse owners or warehouse service providers
- Procurement and packaging partners

**In short:** Every business entity on the platform is an **Associate**.

---

## Operator (Platform Role)
Operators are **people who manage and support associates** inside the OBAOL system.  
Operators work with companies, handle day‑to‑day platform execution, and help coordinate workflows.

**Operators can be:**
- Internal OBAOL operators
- External professionals working on behalf of OBAOL
- Remote staff from anywhere in the world

**In short:** Operators help run the platform and manage associate workflows.

---

## Quick Decision Guide
- You represent a **company** → **Associate**
- You manage/operate companies on the platform → **Operator**

If you’re unsure, start with **Associate**. You can always be connected to an operator later.
