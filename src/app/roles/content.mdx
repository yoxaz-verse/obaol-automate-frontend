# Roles on OBAOL Supreme

OBAOL is built around two core roles. Each role has a clear purpose in the trade execution ecosystem.

## Choose Your Role

- **Associate** → You represent a **company** (trade, logistics, warehouse, procurement, packaging, customs, etc.).
- **Operator** → You **manage and coordinate companies** on the platform.

If you run a business, choose **Associate**. 
If you support business execution and workflows, choose **Operator**.
