# Disclaimer & Engagement Terms

This page outlines important information regarding how OBAOL Supreme operates, what we provide, and the limits of our responsibility.  
By accessing or engaging with OBAOL, you acknowledge and agree to the terms described below.

---

## Nature of OBAOL Supreme

OBAOL Supreme is a **verified agro trade ecosystem** designed to support real-world agricultural and commodity trade.

OBAOL is **not**:
- A marketplace
- A listing website
- A financial trading platform
- A broker or counterparty
- A software-only SaaS product

OBAOL operates as a **trade support and execution ecosystem**, combining verification, coordination, technology, and partner services to support genuine trade activity.

---

## Free Ecosystem Access

Access to the OBAOL ecosystem is **free**.

This includes:
- Viewing and engaging within the verified ecosystem
- Participation by verified suppliers, traders, and partners
- Access to ecosystem tools, communication, and engagement features

OBAOL does **not** charge for:
- Sign-up
- Listings
- Basic participation
- Discovery or visibility within the ecosystem

---

## Execution-Based Engagement

OBAOL may engage more deeply when a **real trade moves toward execution**.

Execution support may include:
- Trade coordination
- Verification support
- Procurement assistance
- Logistics and packaging partner coordination
- Execution continuity and follow-ups

Any execution-based engagement is **context-specific** and occurs only when:
- Trade intent is genuine
- Execution support adds value
- All involved parties are aware of the engagement scope

OBAOL does **not** impose automatic charges or hidden fees.

---

## No Guarantee of Outcomes

OBAOL does not guarantee:
- Trade success
- Profits or margins
- Price realization
- Buyer or seller performance
- Timelines beyond agreed coordination support

All commercial decisions, pricing, and contractual obligations remain solely between the trading parties.

---

## Verification Disclaimer

Verification conducted by OBAOL is intended to **reduce execution risk**, not eliminate it.

Verification:
- Is contextual and trade-specific
- Does not replace due diligence, contracts, or legal safeguards
- Should not be interpreted as a guarantee of counterparty behavior

Participants are responsible for exercising independent judgment.

---

## Logistics & Partner Services Disclaimer

OBAOL works with **trusted logistics, packaging, and service partners** to support trade execution when required.

OBAOL:
- Does not operate as a logistics carrier
- Does not own inventory
- Does not physically handle goods

Final operational decisions and contracts with service providers remain the responsibility of the trading parties.

Technology is used as an **enabler**, not a decision-maker.
