# Associate Role — Who Can Be an Associate

Associates are **companies and business entities** using OBAOL Supreme to trade, execute, and manage commodity workflows.
If you represent a company, you are an Associate.

## Who Can Be an Associate?
Any company involved in the commodity trade lifecycle can join as an Associate, including:

- **Traders** (buyers, sellers, brokers)
- **Warehouses & cold storage providers**
- **Logistics providers** (inland, ocean, air)
- **Customs clearance agencies**
- **Procurement partners**
- **Packaging & inspection partners**

## What Associates Do on OBAOL
Associates use the platform to:
- Manage enquiries and orders
- Track inventory and execution
- Coordinate documentation and compliance
- Access markets and buyers/suppliers globally

**Associates represent the business side of OBAOL.**

## When to Choose Associate
- You operate a company involved in trade or execution
- You provide warehousing, logistics, or procurement services
- You want to onboard products and trade through OBAOL

**If you represent a company, you are an Associate.**
